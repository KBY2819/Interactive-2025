Steppin’ in rhythm, one by one,

Four men walk under London’s sun.

Crossin’ the lines, cool and free,

A moment frozen, history.

Barefoot strides and suits so neat,

Black and white beneath their feet.

Through the city, through the sound,

This road’s forever Abbey-bound.

Cars stand still, the world takes note,

A single shot, a lasting quote.

Side by side, they march in time,

Timeless legends in their prime.
